About Me

I'm Declan Cullen, a passionate game designer hailing from Essex, UK. With a degree in game design from the University of Suffolk, I've delved deep into the world of game development, particularly focusing on crafting games with intriguing procedural elements.

My game design philosophy centers on creating captivating gameplay experiences that are both challenging and unpredictable. This drive for innovation and immersion leads me to incorporate dynamic systems, such as procedural generation techniques like Perlin noise and sine waves, to craft unique and aesthetically pleasing game worlds.

When I'm not immersed in the world of game design, I find inspiration in history and mythology. These passions often find their way into my projects, adding layers of depth and storytelling to the player experience. One notable example is "The King's Servant," a project where historical and mythological elements merge to create a rich and immersive narrative.
