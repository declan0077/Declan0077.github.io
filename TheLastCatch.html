The Last Catch

A strategic puzzle game about sustainable fishing, combining Minesweeper mechanics with environmental awareness.

Overview

The Last Catch merges strategic puzzle gameplay with a compelling focus on real-world environmental challenges. As players explore the depths of the ocean, they learn about the delicate balance of marine ecosystems and the impact of human activity on marine life.

Starting as a traditional fishing game concept, it evolved into a unique blend that combines Minesweeper logic with meaningful storytelling elements that engage players in thought-provoking challenges about sustainability.

Development Journey

Initial Concept

The initial idea focused on creating a traditional fishing game with standard upgrades and gear enhancements. However, the team quickly pivoted to emphasize sustainable practices and the broader environmental impact of fishing.

Iterative Design

Extensive feedback sessions and iterative testing led to the introduction of Minesweeper-inspired mechanics. The emphasis shifted towards balancing entertainment and education, making sure the player experience remained engaging while informative.

Environmental Focus

Incorporating real-world environmental challenges, such as oil spills, rogue fishing boats, and climate change, deepened the game’s impact, offering players insights into the realities of marine conservation.

Key Features

Dynamic Events

Face unique, real-world-inspired challenges such as environmental disasters and rogue fishing practices that test your strategic skills.

Multiple Perspectives

Engage with a variety of characters, each representing different facets of the fishing industry. Your choices shape the relationships and the unfolding narrative.

Educational Value

Learn about sustainable fishing practices, marine conservation efforts, and the importance of protecting aquatic ecosystems through gameplay.

Strategic Depth

Balance competing interests, manage resources carefully, and protect marine life as you tackle quotas and environmental impact head-on.

Gameplay

Core Mechanics

  • • Explore an ocean environments using Minesweeper-style mechanics to find where endangered fish are and mark them to avoid bycatch and maximize yield.
  • • Manage relationships with different groups, each with different interactions that will force you to play in a different style to keep their relation high.
  • • Adapt to ever-changing conditions and environmental events that challenge your strategic planning skills.
