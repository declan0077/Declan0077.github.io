About Declan Cullen

Hello, I’m Declan Cullen, a game designer based in the UK. My journey into game design started at South Essex College and the University of Ipswich, where I developed a deep passion for creating immersive digital experiences. I’m proficient in using game development platforms like Unreal Engine and Unity, and I’ve always been drawn to the intricacies of procedural generation and systems design. I find the challenge of crafting complex, dynamic environments both exhilarating and rewarding.

Currently, I’m pursuing a master’s degree in Game Design at Falmouth University. This has allowed me to work on projects like Arkcarmic Descent, an adventure game set in a labyrinthine complex. I love designing intricate game worlds that challenge players and immerse them in rich narratives. Creating these detailed, engaging spaces is a way for me to explore and express my creativity.

Another project I’m particularly proud of is IVR Safety Training. This project allowed me to combine my interest in gamification with educational content, enhancing learning through immersive virtual reality. I’m passionate about finding innovative ways to apply game design principles to real-world problems, and this project showcased how gamification can make learning more engaging and effective.
